Some Rules of this Quiz
1. You will have only 15 seconds per each question.
2. Once you select your answer, it can't be undone.
3. You can't select any option once time goes off.
4. You can't exit from the Quiz while you're playing.
5. You'll get points on the basis of your correct answers.
Advice For The Quiz
-Use Your Lifelines Wisely : Lifelines like " : Ask the Audience" , " : Phone a Friend" and " : 50/50" can be very helpful. Save them for when you're really stuck.
-Eliminate Wrong Answers : If you're not sure, try to eliminate obviously wrong answers. This can improve your chances even if you're unsure of the correct answer.
-Take Your Time : Don't rush, Take a moment to think through the question and your options before answering.
-Stay Calm : Nerves can affect your performance Try to stay calm and focused.
-Winning and Scoring : Your score will be based on the number of correct answers. The more correct answers you have, the higher your score will be.
